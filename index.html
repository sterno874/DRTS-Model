Mechanism Alpha DaRT intratumoral therapy

Fact Ra-224 seeds implanted in tumor release alpha-emitting daughters that diffuse ~mm-scale, delivering high-LET DNA damage locally (PubMed 31759075).

Educational schematic Interactive modules below explain α penetration, LET, decay chain, hypoxia, and seed placement. Not clinical treatment planning or Monte Carlo dosimetry — see Explain tab for methodology links.

Sim A Penetration depth in tissue

Fact α particles from Ra-224 daughters travel ~40–70 μm in water/tissue (CSDA); β and γ/X-ray penetrate mm–cm. Model Log-scale bars + animated tracks from a seed — slider varies α energy (MeV).

Healthy tissue beyond ~100 μm receives negligible α dose — spatial precision.

Fact CSDA range at 6.8 MeV ≈ 56 μm in water (ICRU/NIST tables). Typical Po-216 α ≈ 6.78 MeV.

Sources: Arazi et al. Phys Med Biol 2010 · Keisari 2007 · ICRU Report 49 stopping powers · IAEA alpha therapy review

Sim B Energy deposition along α track (LET)

Pedagogical schematic LET rises toward end of track (Bragg-like peak) — dense ionization causes complex DNA damage. Not a full Monte Carlo simulation.

LET ≈ 180 (relative units)

Sources: ICRU Report 37 (LET definitions) · Kelson & Keisari 2015 review · Arazi FIH DaRT

Sim C Ra-224 decay chain timeline

Fact Short-lived α-emitting daughters (Rn-220, Po-216) decay near the seed; longer Pb-212/Bi-212 extend the dose cloud ~mm. Half-lives from NNDC.

Ra-224 · t½ 3.66 d · Parent in seed

Sources: NNDC decay data · Arazi 2010 dosimetry · Alpha Tau technology

Sim D Hypoxia independence

Fact Hypoxic tumor cores resist low-LET photon RT (oxygen fixation effect). High-LET α causes direct DNA double-strand breaks with less O₂ dependence (Keisari 2007).

Alpha (DaRT)

High LET, ~40–70 μm α track, oxygen-independent mechanism — spares distant organs when seeds placed correctly.

Beta / Gamma (EBRT)

Lower LET, mm–cm penetration — standard external beam; hypoxic regions less sensitive.

  • Arazi et al. / FIH SCC-HN — 78.6% CR in 28 lesions, no grade ≥3 toxicity
  • Keisari 2007 — preclinical solid tumor responsiveness
  • Early Abscopal / immunity — preclinical + case reports; combo trials planned

Educational schematic only. Does not compute patient-specific dose, prescription, or treatment-planning metrics. Uncertainty in α range ±~20% by energy and tissue composition.

Honesty check: REGAIN interim n=3 local control does not prove systemic GBM control or OS benefit. Do not extrapolate skin SCC FIH CR rate to ReSTART pivotal without discount.
