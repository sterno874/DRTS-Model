## Clinical data highlights (primary-sourced)

### ReSTART / skin SCC
- Pivotal endpoint: **ORR** (confirmed BOR per RECIST 1.1) + **DOR at 6 months** — [NCT05323253](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT05323253).
- Prior first-in-human DaRT SCC/H&N: 78.6% CR, 21.4% PR in 28 lesions; no grade ≥3 toxicity — [PubMed 31759075](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31759075/) (2019; not ReSTART).

### REGAIN / GBM interim (n=3)
- **100% local disease control**, **67% CR** (2 CR + 1 SD with ≥30% reduction) — cutoff May 3, 2026 — ✅ verified [Jun 11 2026 PR](https://alphatau.com/alpha-tau-receives-fda-clearance-to-complete-enrollment-in-regain-recurrent-glioblastoma-trial-and-add-two-u-s-clinical-sites-early-interim-results-showed-100-local-disease-control/).
- One grade 3 SAE (resolved with steroids). **n=3** — ⚠️ not generalizable; Reddit bull cases often omit sample size.

### IMPACT / pancreatic pooled (prior FIH studies)
- Company reported **100% local disease control** in evaluable pancreatic patients at DDW 2026 — ✅ verified in Q1 2026 PR; full peer-reviewed publication ⚠️ pending.
- ASCO 2026 pooled survival data referenced — verify against presentation slides when modeling OS.

### Tolmar collaboration (prostate)
- Tolmar commercializes Alpha DaRT for **prostate (and bladder per agreement scope)** in US; Alpha Tau supplies at **60% of net sales** — ✅ verified [Jun 3 2026 PR](https://alphatau.com/alpha-tau-and-tolmar-announce-strategic-collaboration-to-bring-alpha-dart-therapy-to-u-s-urological-cancer-patients/).
